Company Story

Harry and Harriet's Hang-gliding Haven is one of the leading providers of all things hang-gliding and skydiving related in the Midwest. We are a family-owned business, with locations in both Chicago and Indianapolis. Our top-notch, certified trainers provide our customers with the safest and most enjoyable hang-gliding or skydiving experience possible. All of our locations also include a shop where we sell various kinds of gear and apparel. We pride ourselves on providing avid thrill seakers with a first-class and affordable experience of a lifetime.


Company History

The company was founded by husband and wife Harry and Harriet Johnson in 1995. Both avid and experienced thrill seakers themselves, they decided that they wanted to make a living and career off of this passion. That is when they decided to open Harry and Harriet's Hang-gliding Haven, and they have been loving it ever since. Their original location is just outside the Chicago city limits, where they and their family reside, however they have also added another location just outside Indianapolis. They are looking forward to continuing to expand their operations in the future and continuing to be the leading provider for all things hang-gliding and skydiving related.
